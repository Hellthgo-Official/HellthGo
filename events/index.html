About Health,Finance, Blockchain 2022

The event agenda is to define the real use case of blockchain in the healthcare industry with deliverables of how to make fast and secured payments for health-related issues leveraging a decentralized wallet powered by the blockchain.

In a vision to advance digital health, Health, finance, Blockchain 2022 has been put together to empower healthcare professionals, blockchain enthusiasts, and many others in a collaborative network to meet with counterparts and share growth ideas that revolutionize the African healthcare system.
